My IP Address

Privacy Policy

Effective June 27, 2026

Data use

My IP Address sends HTTPS requests to ipify to retrieve and display your public IPv4 and IPv6 addresses when available. It also sends your IP address to ipwho.is to show an approximate geolocation map. These providers may process request logs under their privacy policies. We do not use this information for tracking, advertising, analytics, or profiling.

Storage

The app does not store your IP address. Refresh interval and time format preferences are stored only on your device and are removed when you delete the app.

Your choices

The app has no accounts. You can stop data processing by not refreshing the address or by deleting the app. For privacy questions or deletion requests, email edman.build@gmail.com.

Changes

Material changes will be published on this page with a new effective date.
